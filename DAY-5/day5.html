Chocolate Viennese Whirls

What's better than Viennese Whirls? Nothing, you say? Well, allow me to introduce chocolate Viennese Whirls!!

Ingredients

  • 250g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 75g icing sugar
  • 225g plain flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 30ml full-fat milk

Filling

  • 75g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 150g icing sugar

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/160°fan and line 2 baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the butter on its own until smooth and creamy. Add the icing sugar and mix until combined. Gradually add the plain flour, cocoa powder and milk until you have a smooth dough.
  3. Spoon the mixture into a piping bag fitted with a star piping tip. Pipe 30 circles of dough onto the lined trays, about 5cm in diameter, leaving a couple of centimetres between each. Bake for 20 minutes. Once baked, leave to cool whilst you make the filling.
  4. Now for the filling. Beat the butter in a clean bowl until smooth. Add the icing sugar and beat again until smooth. I add the icing sugar a bit at a time, to avoid an icing sugar cloud!! Spread a small amount of buttercream filling onto one biscuit and sandwich with another biscuit.
