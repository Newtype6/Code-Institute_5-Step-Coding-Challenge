Chocolate Viennese Whirls

add image here

What's better than Viennese Whirls? Nothing, you say? Well, allow me to introduce chocolate Viennese Whirls!!

Ingredients

  • 250g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 75g icing sugar
  • 225g plain flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 30ml full-fat milk

Filling

  • 75g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 150g icing sugar
